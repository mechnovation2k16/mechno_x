2. Design, Fabrication, Control of Hypersonic Aircrafts and Recent Trends in Aeronautics (Completed)    
Field:    Aerospace Engineering
About the Workshop:    
Prelude:  
  •       The development in the aerospace industry has always been in the form of the race for higher speeds and lower chances of failure at extreme speeds. With hypersonic aircrafts, there are various unforeseeable parameters and loopholes, which may pose difficulties, before, during and after the operation. Hence, all the aspects involving hypersonic aircrafts must be insusceptible to human errors.
Date:    September 3, 2016
Time:    9 AM to 4 PM
Registration Fee:   ₹600 (includes food and refreshments)
About the Speakers:    
  • Forenoon:   Design, Fabrication, Control of Hypersonic Aircrafts

          Dr. S. Elangovan, Professor and Dean of the Department of Aeronautical Engineering, Bharath University, is a prominent figure in the research of supersonic and hypersonic vehicles in India. He has several DRDO-funded research projects under his name. He has nearly 30 years of experience teaching the subject to young minds and around 10 years of experience as a research scientist. He has published several papers in top international journals and has been the key figure in numerous conferences. Currently, he is involved in an indigenous research project sponsored by the DRDO.

  • Afternoon:   Recent Trends in Aeronautics

          Prof. V. Kanagarajan, F. AeSI, is a leading research enthusiast in the field of Experimental Aerodynamics. He has several accolades to his name, related with Aerospace and Aeronautical Engineering, having served as the Chairman of AeSI (Aero Society of India - Chennai Branch). He was a former Director Grade Scientist of top government funded projects at NTAF, under NAL, Bangalore. Furthermore, he was the former holder of the prestigious KAVP Memorial AR & DB Chair and is the current holder of Prof. KAV. Pandalai Chair at MIT, Chennai.
Payment Details:    The fee of ₹600 is to be deposited to Ac. No. 610991757 (Indian Bank), Bank IFSC code:IDIB000S107 (Peelamedu Branch) as soon as possible. On-spot payment and on-spot registration is also available.
